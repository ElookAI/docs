# Quickstart Guide

Welcome to the quickstart guide! This will walk you through the basic steps to get started with our platform.

## Signup Process

1. Go to the signup page
2. Enter your details
3. Click "Create Account"

![Signup Page](placeholder.png)

## Login Process  

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your credentials
3. Click "Login"

![Login Page](placeholder.png)

## Accessing the Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll be redirected to your dashboard where you can:

- View key metrics
- Access different sections
- Manage your account

![Dashboard Overview](placeholder.png)

## Next Steps

Now that you're set up, you can explore the platform's features. Check out our documentation for more advanced usage.
